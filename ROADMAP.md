# 🗺️ Roadmap

Building this in public. Here's where it stands.

## ✅ Done
- Walkable 3D campus with a third-person Roblox-style character (walk + run)
- 16 strategy boards based on _How to Win at College_, with a walk-up-to-learn mechanic
- Progress tracking + graduation celebration (confetti)
- Talkable NPCs with funny dialogue (Tommy Trojan, professors, club recruiter, tour guide)
- Pixel-art textures, palm trees, clock tower, Trojan + dog statues, Metro train
- Roblox-style game UI + generated sound (footsteps, learn chime, ambient birds) with mute

## 🐞 Known issues
- **Conversation box and lesson board can open at the same time** and overlap on screen — they should take turns so only one panel is ever up. *(Top priority.)*
- No collision — you can walk through statues, students, and props.
- Camera can clip into objects in tight spots.

## 🔭 Planned / ideas
**Make it feel like a game, not a walking sim with billboards:**
- Make signs feel like *discoveries* (glow/reveal as you approach) instead of a wall of billboards.
- Fill the world: students on benches, bikes, dogs, coffee carts, club booths, a fountain, banners.
- Add a clear spawn landmark you *want* to walk toward (bigger tower / library dome / fountain).
- Vary the buildings (dorm, library, student union, engineering, business school, stadium) so they read as different places.
- Calmer UI: a "Freshman Year — 8 / 16 Lessons Found" header, with controls that auto-hide after ~10s.

**Lean into the "Spoiled Children" satire:**
- Luxury dorms, gold statues, sports cars parked outside dorms
- A champagne fountain, a "TUITION: $98,000/YEAR" welcome banner
- A mascot dog wearing sunglasses

**Presentation:**
- A proper hero-shot thumbnail (low camera, character facing you, archway + confetti + crowd, big title) — not a raw screenshot.
